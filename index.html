About Us

Osteonix Solutions, LLC provides premium engineering consulting services at the interface of technology and manufacturing. We help clients around the world optimize product design, improve manufacturing processes, and diagnose product problems. Our methods are based on technical fundamentals – employing grounded knowledge of mechanics, physics, chemistry, material science, electricity and magnetism, and manufacturing to produce practical, useful results. Our consultants' backgrounds encompass industrial experience, research, teaching for some of the world’s best engineering schools, and publishing. At Osteonix , we work to understand your needs, uses, purposes, and goals before proposing a solution or embarking on a program. We won’t just answer your questions—we solve your problems.

Mission

Our mission is simple: We aim to be the best in the world for testing, failure analysis, materials science, modeling, and simulation of nonlinear, coupled problems, so that our clients can deliver their best products to their customers. We work every day to realize this mission and solve our clients’ problems, guided by our commitment
